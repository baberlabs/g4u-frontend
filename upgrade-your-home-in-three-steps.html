Upgrade your home in three steps

1

Book a call

Discover your eligibility

Connect with us to find out if you're eligible for free home upgrades under the ECO scheme. Our friendly team will guide you through the eligibility criteria and answer any questions you might have.

What to Expect: A brief conversation to understand your needs and explain how the ECO scheme can benefit you.

Why It's Important: Ensures you’re on the right path to making your home energy-efficient at no cost.

Book your call now →
2

Free home retrofit assessment

Tailored home energy evaluation

Once eligible, we’ll arrange a comprehensive retrofit home assessment at your convenience. Our experts will evaluate your home to identify the best energy-saving upgrades tailored to your specific needs.

What to Expect: A detailed inspection of your home to pinpoint areas for improvement.

Why It's Important: Maximizes the efficiency and impact of the upgrades, ensuring they meet your home's unique requirements.

3

Hassle-free installation

Seamless upgrade implementation

Our trusted partners will carry out all the necessary installations and upgrades, transforming your home with minimal disruption to you. From paperwork to project completion, we handle it all.

What to Expect: Professional installation of selected energy-efficient measures by certified experts.

Why It's Important: Elevates your home’s energy efficiency and comfort while reducing your carbon footprint — all executed seamlessly and stress-free.

An independent retrofit coordinator will guide the entire upgrade process, following government guidelines, to ensure you get the maximum benefits for your home.
