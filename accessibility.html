Accessibility statement

At Grants For You, we are committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards to ensure we provide equal access to all users.

Efforts to support accessibility

Grants For You takes the following measures to ensure accessibility:

  • Continuous monitoring: Our website is regularly scanned to identify and fix potential accessibility issues.
  • Training: We train our staff on accessibility issues and web accessibility guidelines.
  • Clear accessibility goals: We strive to comply with WCAG 2.1 level AA standards as a minimum level of accessibility.
  • Feedback: We welcome feedback from users regarding the accessibility of our website.

Technical specifications

Accessibility of Grants For You relies on the following technologies to work with the particular combination of web browser and any assistive technologies or plugins installed on your computer:

  • HTML
  • WAI-ARIA
  • CSS
  • JavaScript

These technologies are relied upon for conformance with the accessibility standards used.

Accessibility features

Our website includes the following accessibility features:

  • Alternative text for images: All images contain alternative text for screen reader users.
  • Keyboard navigation: Our website can be navigated using a keyboard.
  • Contrast options: Users can adjust text and background color contrast to improve readability.
  • Text size adjustment: Users can adjust the text size through their browser settings.

Feedback and contact

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of Grants For You. Please let us know if you encounter accessibility barriers on Grants For You:

  • Email: contact@grants4you.org
  • Phone: 0121 728 5644
  • Postal Address: 156 Great Charles Street, Queensway, Birmingham, B3 3HN

We try to respond to feedback within 3 business days.

External resources

For more information on web accessibility and legal requirements, here are some resources:

  • Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG)
  • Section508.gov
  • Accessibility Initiative (WAI)

Grants For You is committed to creating an accessible and inclusive digital environment. We strive to comply with all applicable standards and go beyond to provide an accessible experience for all users.
